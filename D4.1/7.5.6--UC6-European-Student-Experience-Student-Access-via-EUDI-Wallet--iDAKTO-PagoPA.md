### UC 6: European Student Experience/Student Access via EUDI Wallet (iDAKTO-PagoPA)

In UC6, the Trust Framework establishes the common rules and technical standards that allow foreign universities to trust credentials issued by Italian or French institutions. Relying Parties are able to verify Student Card and provide services, since Issuers are considered authorized academic entities. 
This framework allows student to verify their status ensuring the authenticity of their attestation across different European countries.
