#### Selective Disclosure

In UC7, the principle of **Selective Disclosure** is fundamental, as it enables passengers to verify their identity and eligibility without disclosing unnecessary sensitive information. This approach ensures **Data Minimization**, allowing Trenitalia to confirm the passenger's status while protecting their privacy.

Specifically, users can present their primary identification data and their Disability Card status without revealing details irrelevant to the use case, such as their home address or the specific nature of their disability. Instead, the process focuses exclusively on the certified right to access a discount or the "_companion entitlement_." This ensures that Trenitalia receives only the essential attributes required to authorize the service, maintaining a high standard of privacy and trust throughout the transaction.
