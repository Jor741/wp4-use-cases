#### Revocation
Revocation
- ETA credentials have a limited lifetime of several years. 
- ETA credentials can be revoked or cancelled by the issuing QEAA provider if a holder's presence is deemed not conducive to the public good, such as for security reasons, criminal convictions, or immigration breaches.
- In that case the issuing QEAA provider will publish the revoked ETA on its revocation list. This list can be consulted by the relying party receiving the ETA.
