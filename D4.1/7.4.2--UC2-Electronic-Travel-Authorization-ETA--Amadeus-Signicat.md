### UC 2: Electronic Travel Authorization (ETA) (Amadeus)
Selective Disclosure are key GDPR requirements that we have to take into account.

Selective Disclosure
- In this use case selective disclosure does not apply.
  
User Consent
- When applying for an ETA, the traveller must accept a privacy information notice that outlines how personal data is collected, used, and shared by the ETA-issuing QEAA.  
- During the application process, the traveller must agree to the privacy notice to proceed.
