### UC 2: Electronic Travel Authorization (ETA) (Amadeus-Signicat)

Issuance and verification flow.
Here is the technical flow for the issuance of an ETA attestation. The assumption here is that PID is already on the wallet and is used to login to the ETA Credential Issuer. The ETA Credential Issuer utilizes ReadID Ready application to obtain passport-based information and a ReadID Server to verify the NFC-read data. Optionally the ReadID server may also compare PID info with the passport read personal data, ask ETA specific questions or do identity document holder binding verification. Is issued ETA credential will be issued to the wallet. Via the wallet, the user can the ETA credential share with interested parties (e.g. Amadeus, not shown).
