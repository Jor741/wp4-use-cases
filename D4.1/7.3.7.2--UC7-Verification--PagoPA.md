#### Verification

While the issuance process is a prerequisite, the verification phase represents the core of UC7. To enable passengers to personalize fares and access discounts, Trenitalia requires the verification of both the **PID and the European Disability Card** via the EUDI Wallet.

To ensure seamless system interoperability, Trenitalia, acting as the Relying Party, verifies these credentials in compliance with the OpenID4VP standard. This allows passengers to share only the necessary data points through Selective Disclosure, protecting user privacy.

_Scenario Execution_
UC7 focuses exclusively on the **remote verification** of the PID and Disability Card within the Trenitalia app during the booking phase, prior to payment finalization. The process unfolds as follows:
- **Remote Sharing**: the passenger initiates the sharing process via their EUDI Wallet, providing a Verifiable Attestation of their Disability Card.
- **Automated Validation**: the platform automatically validates the attributes and applies the corresponding discount.
- **Companion Entitlement**: if the credential includes a "companion entitlement", the system recognizes the eligibility for an additional passenger and requests their basic information (name and surname).

Note: Although this flow uses the Disability Card as the primary use case, the underlying logic is applicable to any other verifiable attribute.
