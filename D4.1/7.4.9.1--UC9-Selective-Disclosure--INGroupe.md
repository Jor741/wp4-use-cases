#### Selective Disclosure
Selective disclosure in UC9 is a data‑minimisation property of the presentation itself: different relying parties request different evidence depending on their operational role, and the wallet releases only the attributes or derived proofs required for that specific transaction. 

Traveller is not asked to disclose the same full dataset at every airport touchpoint. A security check point for example will require only identity and biometrics data while the baggage drop will require flight information.

In summary, selective disclosure in UC9 is not always a full release of credential contents but a context‑specific disclosure model aligned with necessity, proportionality, and operational need.
