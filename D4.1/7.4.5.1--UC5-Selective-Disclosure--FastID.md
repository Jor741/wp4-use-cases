#### Selective Disclosure

UC5 follows the principle of Selective Disclosure when the wallet interacts with the relying party for issuing or confirming the access credential.

The verifier does not need to receive the full identity dataset of the wallet. Only the minimum attributes required to issue or validate the stadium access credential are shared through the wallet flow.

The access credential itself does not rely on additional selective disclosure at presentation time, as it is already derived from the PID/DTC and only includes the information necessary to grant access (such as access rights and validity). The wallet interaction is therefore mainly about controlling what is shared during issuance, not during every access event.
