## Sector-Specific Regulations

Beyond the horizontal layer of eIDAS 2.0 (@sec-6_1), every WP4 use case is also subject to a body of sector-specific regulations that applies because of the sector in which the use case operates. This section consolidates that body of regulation in a single place, drawing on the input provided by the UC leads responding to WP2 needs.

Each row reports an instrument as it appears in the UC-collected regulations list and indicates the WP4 use cases on which it bears most directly. The applicability columns present in the source workbook (issuance, issuance protocol, presentation protocol, format, trust models, legal value, data protection, cybersecurity, sovereignty) are summarised in the *Notes* column when they carry information beyond the instrument's general purpose. The status reflects what the workbook records at the closure of T4.2.
