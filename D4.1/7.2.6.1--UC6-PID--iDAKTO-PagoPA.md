#### PID (Personal Identification Data)

In UC6, the **PID** is used to establish and verify the identity of the student and match it with Student card data. 
It is issued by an authorised **PID provider** (Isituto Poligrafico e Zecca dello stato for Italy) in accordance with **eIDAS 2.0** requirements and stored in the EUDIW. It is primarily used during wallet onboarding and, where required in our scenario, to match the student with the existing digital identity or with existing records held by relying parties (e.g. transport subscriptions or university systems).
The intended verifiers of PID are relying parties that require identity matching in addition to status verification. However, in many UC6 scenarios, full PID disclosure is not necessary.
