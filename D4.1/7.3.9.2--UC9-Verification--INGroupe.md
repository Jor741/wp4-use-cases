#### Verification
This use case tests two types of verification: 

Remote verification: before issuing the traveller biometric profile or ready to travel attestation, IN Groupe solution for the Marseille Airport acting as Relying Party needs to verify attributes from PID, PhotoID, and Digital Travel Credential by initiating a remote verification request. The traveller uses their EUDIW to share their attributes and confirm their action using biometrics to approve the sharing. The RP receives the Verifiable Credential and verifies its integrity, using the RP’s public key and it finally issues the biometric profile or ready to travel attestation. 

Proximity verification: Marseille Airports verification points at bag drop, security checkpoints and boarding gate will verify traveller credentials through proximity sharing option from a EUWI wallet (ISO 18013-5) or by scanning the biometric QR code attributes.
