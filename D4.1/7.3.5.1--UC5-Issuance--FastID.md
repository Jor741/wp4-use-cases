#### Issuance

In UC5 the issuance flow starts when the fan or employee creates an N.E.C. account and onboard into the FastID wallet. Before access credentials can be issued, the user completes an identity verification process by scanning their passport and performing a biometric identity check. Based on this process, the wallet creates the PID and the DTC.

The issuance of the PID and DTC is handled through the FastID wallet and follows the general OpenID4VCI approach used in the EUDIW ecosystem. After the identity is verified, a football ticket or stadium access credential is issued to the wallet and linked to the verified identity of the holder.

The access credential is then stored inside the wallet and can later be presented at stadium accespoints to perform verification. Depending on the scenario, the credential may contain information such as access rights, validity period, and entitlement type (fan, employee, student or similar roles).
