### UC 7: Accessing Discounting Train Fares via EUDIW (PagoPA)

For UC7, the Trust Framework establishes the legal and technical foundation that enables private service providers, such as Trenitalia, to securely accept digital identity credentials. This ecosystem relies on the inherent trustworthiness of credentials provided by certified Issuers, ensuring that discounts and personalized fares are granted based on verified, tamper-proof information.
