## UC 7: Accessing Discounted Train Fares via EUDIW (PagoPA)
UC7 aims to streamline the train booking process for passengers eligible for specific concessions, such as, for instance, disability, student or age-related discounts, by leveraging digital credentials. For instance, a passenger with a certified disability can easily obtain a reduced-fare ticket or request travel assistance directly on the Trenitalia app through his EUDIW. 
This process removes the burden of manual data entry and physical document or certificates verification, ensuring a frictionless user experience. 
The ultimate goal is to scale this model at a European level: since these credentials are designed for EU-wide recognition, Italian passengers should enjoy the same seamless experience with any European rail operator, effectively demonstrating the power of cross-border interoperability.
