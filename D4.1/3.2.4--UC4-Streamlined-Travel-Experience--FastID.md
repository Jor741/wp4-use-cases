### UC 4: Streamlined Travel Experience Fo Visitors (FastID)

The UC 4.4 use case has been developed with a small group of parties working on digital identity and access control.

FastID acts as both the wallet provider and the lead of the use case. It is responsible for the overall setup, including identity verification and how credentials are issued and used.

The focus of the scenario is on employees who need access to certain areas, using digital credentials instead of a physical badge.

The relying party role, where access is actually checked, is handled within the project setup. The scenario is tested in a controlled environment at Amsterdam Airport, to reflect a realistic airport setting without being part of a live operational system.

Other actors are involved in issuing the PID and creating the Digital Travel Credential (DTC), which is linked to the access rights.

Technical partners support the connections between the different steps, from registration to access validation.

Work on this use-case is mainly done through:

Short working sessions to define the flow,
Shared documents to keep track of changes

The goal is to show how digital credentials can be used for employee access in a practical setting, without making the process more complex than it needs to be.
