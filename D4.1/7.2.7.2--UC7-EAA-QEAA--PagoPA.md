#### Electronic Attestations of Attributes (EAA / QEAA)
Another credential useful in the use case is the **Disability Card**, as an example of credential used to testify a specific status, such as the disability, for the eligibility to a certain personalized fare. In this case, the verification of the disability card enables the passenger to gain a discounted ticket and a free ticket for the companion passenger. As the PID, the Disability Card is issued by the Italian official Public Issuer, Istituto Poligrafico e Zecca dello Stato and it is verified by Trenitalia, acting as a Relying Party. 

_Data model for Disability Card_
- family_name
- given_name
- birth_date
- portrait
- card serial number
- expiry_date
- Indication of entitlement to an accompanying person or additional support (displayed only for eligible holders)
- QR code
