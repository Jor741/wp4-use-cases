### UC 6: European Student Experience/Student Access via EUDI Wallet

UC6 aims to demonstrate a seamless cross-border experience for students by integrating existing national services with the EUDIW. In this scenario, for accessing personalized services (e.g. univerisy access or public transport discounts), both Italian and French students can verify their status by presenting their **Verifiable Credentials** on their EUDIW. For this purpose, the attestations needed are:
- **PID**;
- **European Student Card**.
