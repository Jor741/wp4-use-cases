### UC 8: Overnight Hospitality & Cross-border Train Journey

**UC8** focuses on the overnight cross-border journey, enabling passengers to use their EUDIW for a real-time identity verification across national borders.

Specifically, this UC pilots the remote and on-site verification of the PID and/or DTC via the EUDI Wallet, in full compliance with OpenID4VP standards. 

In this scenario, the **issuance** of credentials is a precondition, assuming that travelers have already provisioned their attestations. Similarly, although not the primary focus, **revocation** remains an essential requirement to guarantee that only valid, up-to-date credentials are used to grant access to cross-border transit services.
