#### Credential Issuance for the *Board the Flight* Use Case

In practical terms, the result of this issuance step is that the traveller’s wallet holds at least:

- PID, already obtained from a trusted identity issuer; and/or
- DTC, already obtained from a trusted identity issuer;

**Issuance Flow (Illustration purpose only)**

1. Traveler authenticates to the **National EUDI Wallet**
2. National authority verifies identity 
3. Authority issues:
   - **PID** as root identity credential
   - **(Q)EAA**, including **DTC**
4. Credentials are **cryptographically signed by the authority**
5. PID and (Q)EAA are stored in the **EUDI Wallet**
