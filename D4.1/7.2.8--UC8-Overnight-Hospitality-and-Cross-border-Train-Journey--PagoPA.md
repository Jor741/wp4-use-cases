### UC 8: Overnight Hospitality & Cross-border Train Journey (PagoPA)
UC8 aims to verify the identity of the passenger both during the online booking of the ticket and on the train, in proximity, during the overnight journey from Rome to Monaco. The identity verification is also aimed to reserve women dedicated cabins for the night.  For this purpose, in this UC, Trenitalia verifies mostly the PID, but it could also verify the DTC.
