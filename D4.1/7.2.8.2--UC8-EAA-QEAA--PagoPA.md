#### Electronic Attestations of Attributes (EAA / QEAA)
Another possible credential included in UC8 is DTC, that could be verified by Trenitalia in order to match the identity of the passenger with the ticket booked. The issuance of the DTC is responsibility of the Italian Public Issuer, Istituto Poligrafico e Zecca dello Stato, but it follows the evolutions of WP3.
Considering the WP3's ongoing analysis on DTC, the specific Data model for this credential needs to be defined and it is an open point for the UC.
