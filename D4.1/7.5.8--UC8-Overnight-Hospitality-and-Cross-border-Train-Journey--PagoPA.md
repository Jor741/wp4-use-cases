### UC 8: Overnight Hospitality & Cross-border Train Journey (PagoPA)

In UC8, the Trust Framework is essential for maintaining a chain of trust between passengers and national authorities, allowing Trenitalia to recognize and accept credentials regardless of the Member State of origin. During an overnight journey, each verification relies on it to ensure that the passenger’s attributes are issued by an authorized body, providing a secure and unified verification process that remains consistent as the traveler crosses national borders.
