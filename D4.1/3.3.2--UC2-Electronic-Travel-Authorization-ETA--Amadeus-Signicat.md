### UC 2: Electronic Travel Authorization (ETA) (Amadeus)

We used the folllowing engagement methods:
  1. Bilateral calls and alignment meetings,
       - prepared by one of the participants
  2. Working papers and iterative written review, used to consolidate the results of stakeholder discussions into progressively more stable scenario descriptions;
  3. Shared drafting and collaborative document exchange, used to refine use-case text, episode descriptions, and technical assumptions across WP4 contributors and external operational partners.

Overall, stakeholder involvement was an iterative co-process rather than a one-off consultation exercise.
