#### PID (Personal Identification Data)

In UC7, the **PID** is used to verify identity matching between the train ticket data and the user requesting it, ensuring trust during the identity verification process and the application of the discount on the fare.
The issuance of the PID is responsibility of the Italian official Public Issuer, **Istituto Poligrafico e Zecca dello Stato**, and it is a pre-condition for beginning of the scenario: the UC testing will refer only to the remote presentation of the credential and the verification by Trenitalia, acting as a Relying Party. The initial phase of Wallet Istance activation and PID issuance is not included in the scenario. 

In order to ensure the proper identity matching between the user data and the personalized ticket, Trenitalia requires the verification of limited data, that the user will properly share according to the principle of **Selective Disclosure**. The essential data model for the PID is the following:
- family_name
- given_name
- birth_date
- birth_place
- nationality
- gender : although this is an additional attribute, gender is fundamental to reach the scenario objective of verifying passenger identity in order to reserve a women-only cabin.
