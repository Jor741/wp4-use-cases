### UC 6: European Student Experience/Student Access via EUDI Wallet (iDAKTO-PagoPA)

For this use case, the primary reference documents are those related to **eIDAS 2.0 and the EUDIW**. 
In particular, the **ARF** provides a common baseline applicable across all work packages, including WP4-UC6. More specifically, the implementation of this use case relies on the standards and specifications defined under the eIDAS 2.0 framework for wallet issuance, credential management, and verification processes. These ensure alignment with the broader European Digital Identity ecosystem.

At the use case level, there is currently **no single standardised metadata model or data schema for student credentials**. Data structures and attributes remain dependent on the card or credential issuer, reflecting existing national or institutional approaches. However, when credentials are issued to and used within the EUDIW, they follow the common technical and interoperability standards defined by eIDAS 2.0. This enables consistent handling, presentation, and verification of student credentials across services and borders.

Collaborative work is supported through shared document repositories and common templates used across the project, ensuring alignment and consistency between stakeholders.
