### UC 4: Streamlined Travel Experience (FastID)

- **eIDAS 2.0 / European Digital Identity Wallet framework**
Provides the legal and trust framework for issuing, holding, and verifying digital identity credentials across the EU.

- **EUDI Wallet Architecture and Reference Framework (ARF)**
Defines the architecture, roles, and trust model for wallets, issuers, verifiers, and intermediary services in the EUDI Wallet ecosystem.

- **Verifiable Credentials (VC) data model**
Standard data model used to represent PID attributes, Digital Travel Credentials (DTC), and access credentials in a cryptographically verifiable format.

- **OpenID4VCI 1.0**
Protocol used for secure issuance of credentials from issuers into the user’s wallet.

- **OpenID4VP 1.0**
Protocol used for requesting and presenting credentials from the wallet to relying parties for verification.

- **OAuth 2.0**
Underlying authorization framework used in issuance and presentation flows.

- **SD-JWT / SD-JWT-VC**
Credential format enabling selective disclosure of attributes while maintaining cryptographic integrity.

- **ISO/IEC 18013-5 (mDL / mDoc)**
Standard for mobile identity documents, used for secure proximity-based presentation of credentials at physical access points.

- **Status List / Revocation mechanisms (eIDAS 2.0 aligned)**
Mechanisms used to verify whether credentials (PID, DTC, access credentials) are still valid at the time of presentation.

- **EUDI Wallet Intermediary Service pattern**
Architectural component used to support integration between wallet flows and external systems, including trust verification and API-based orchestration.
