#### Verification

**UC6** focuses on two different verifications for the **Student Card**:

- **Remote presentation**, when the student purchases a ticket on the Transportation Service website/app having the right to access personalized discounted fares for transportation. During the purchase, the RP needs to verify the student attestation to apply the discount on the ticket, so the system initiates a remote verification request. The student uses their EUDIW to share their student card attributes and confirm their status using biometrics to approve the sharing. The RP receives the Verifiable Credential and verifies its integrity, using the RP’s public key and it finally applies the discount.
- **Proximity verification** for physical access to restricted areas in university, es campus libraries or buildings in Angers, in which the RP requires an identity verification. The student presents a QR code to interact with the local terminal, which verifies the student status in real-time to grant access.

In both cases, Relying Parties verify the Student Card credential in compliance with the **OPENID4VP Standards**, in order to ensure interoperability and Selective Disclosure for users.
