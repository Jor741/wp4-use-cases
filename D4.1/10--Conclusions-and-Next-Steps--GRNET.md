# 10. Conclusions and Next Steps

This final chapter closes D4.1 with a summary of what has been specified and a forward-looking view of the activities that will carry the work of WP4 into its subsequent phases. It answers two questions: *what has D4.1 actually delivered?* and *what happens next, and when?*

### Position of this chapter in the deliverable

Chapter 10 is deliberately short. The substance of D4.1 is carried by the preceding chapters, from the use case specifications in Chapter 4 to the technical foundations in Chapter 7 and the actor mapping in Chapter 8. The function of Chapter 10 is to *close the loop*: to recap the specified use cases at the right level of abstraction for an executive reader, to capture the open items that must be resolved before the pilot can move forward, and to set out the path to the next two WP4 deliverables, D4.2 (Implementation Manual, due at Milestone 15, M18) and D4.3 (Validation Report, due at Milestone 16, M22).

### Structure of the chapter

The chapter is organised into four sections, each addressing one closing dimension of the deliverable.

* **Section 10.1 — Summary of Specified Use Cases.** Recaps the 11 use cases across the three WP4 scenarios, summarises the key findings of the stock-taking activity, and states the overall readiness level of the pilot for the next phases. The section is written for a reader who has not consulted Chapter 4 in detail and who needs a short, honest, high-level view of the pilot portfolio as it stands at the end of T4.2.
* **Section 10.2 — Open Issues and Outstanding Decisions.** Lists the concrete items that need resolution before or during the implementation phase. This includes pending legal clarifications (for example, around sector-specific regulations identified in Chapter 6), undecided technical choices that UC leads have flagged, coordination items with other Work Packages (WP2 on profile versions, WP3 on DTC integration timelines, WP6 on PBEE Framework availability), and UC specifications whose maturity is uneven at the time of D4.1. The items in this section are complementary to the risks recorded in Section 9.1: a risk is a possible adverse event with a mitigation plan, whereas an open issue is a concrete item awaiting a decision or an action.
* **Section 10.3 — Roadmap Towards D4.2 (Implementation Manual).** Describes the planned activities and timeline for moving from specifications (D4.1) to implementation (D4.2). The section identifies what T4.3 (Implementation Support) will do, the key milestones leading to Milestone 15 at M18, the expected structure and scope of D4.2, and the hand-offs between UC leads, the WP4 coordinator, and the partners contributing to each UC.
* **Section 10.4 — Roadmap Towards D4.3 (Validation Report).** Describes the planned activities for T4.4 (Piloting, User Testing, and UC Evaluation) that will feed into D4.3. The section outlines the testing approach, the KPI framework against which each UC will be evaluated, the feedback-collection mechanisms, and the expected structure of D4.3, culminating in Milestone 16 at M22.
