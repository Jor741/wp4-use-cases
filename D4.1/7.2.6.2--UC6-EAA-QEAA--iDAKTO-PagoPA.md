#### Electronic Attestations of Attributes (EAA / QEAA)

The main credential used in this use case is the **Student Attestation**, provided as an Electronic Attestation of Attributes or, where applicable, a Qualified Electronic Attestation of Attributes (or PUBEAA if public).

This credential is issued by an authorised attestation provider, such as a university or a recognised educational authority (**GYSC for France and Polimi for Italy)** . It confirms the student status of the holder and may include attributes such as _enrolment status, institution, and validity period._

The intended verifiers are **relying parties** offering student-specific services or benefits (e.g. transport operators, cultural venues, accommodation providers). In most cases, only the verification of student status is required.
