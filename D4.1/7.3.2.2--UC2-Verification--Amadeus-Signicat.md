#### Verification

Upon the receipt of the ETA credential from the EUDI Wallet of the traveller (user), the relying party verifies its digital signature via the trusted list infrastrucure.

Verification Pattern

The flow follows an OpenID4VP‑style pattern:

- The Amadeus backend system acting as verifier initiates a request for API‑relevant attributes.
- The request is delivered to the traveler’s EUDI Wallet via a supported online engagement method (redirect, deep link, or QR).
- The wallet displays:
  - The requested digital ETA credential 
  - The purpose of the request (API collection)
  - The minimum data to be disclosed
- The traveler authenticates locally to the wallet and consents to disclosure.
- The wallet constructs a verifiable presentation and returns it to the verifier (directly or via an intermediary service).
- The verifier performs:
  - Issuer trust and signature validation
  - Validity and status checks
  - Policy checks specific to API requirements
- The verifier issues an autorisation token to the Amadeus backend.
