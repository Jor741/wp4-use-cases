#### Revocation

As with issuance, revocation is not the primary focus of UC7; however, it remains an integral part of the scenario. It ensures that Trenitalia can rely on trusted, up-to-date credentials when applying discounts or personalizing fares.

In accordance with the EUDI Wallet framework, the Relying Party must verify the validity of the data before providing services. This is achieved by checking the Status List associated with the credentials. Since the Italian Public Issuer (IPZS) manages the entire credential lifecycle, any credential that is no longer valid—whether due to expiration, revocation, or other administrative reasons—will result in a failed verification. Consequently, the passenger will be unable to access the personalized fare within the app.
