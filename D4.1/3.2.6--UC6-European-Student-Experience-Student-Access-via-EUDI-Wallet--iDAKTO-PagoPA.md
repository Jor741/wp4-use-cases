### UC 6: European Student Experience/Student Access via EUDI Wallet (iDAKTO-PagoPA)

UC6 scenario is focusing on Students status and student cards. For the design, deployment and development of the scenario, multiple actors from Italy and France have contributed: Wallet Providers, Technology Providers, Relying Parties.

- **ANTS** is acting as a Wallet Provider for France;
- **iDAKTO** is acting as Tecnhology Provider in France, supporting ANTS;
- **GYSC** is acting as Student Card Issuer for France;
- **University of Angers** is acting as a French Relying Party, verifying the Student Card;
- **PagoPA** is acring as a Wallet Provider for Italy;
- **Istituto Poligrafico e Zecca dello Stato** acting as the italian official issuer for PID;
- **Politecnico di Milano** is acting as the Italian Student Card Issuer.

This scenarario is the result of similar UC ideas both in France and in Italy, for this reason it is enabled by the cooperation between actors across the country. To ensure clear engagement for all stakeholders, UC6 has been analyzed through:
- **Individual work and progress**, used to explore national landscape and processes
- **Bilateral calls** or overall calls to share progress, ask questions, and move to the next steps.
- **Shared collaborative documents**, used to define the content of deliverables before transferring them on NextCloud.
