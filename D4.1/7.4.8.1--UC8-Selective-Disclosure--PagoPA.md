#### Selective Disclosure

Similarly to UC7, UC8 strictly adheres to the principle of **Selective Disclosure**, ensuring that passengers share only the data points strictly necessary for identity verification. This approach eliminates the exposure of sensitive information that is irrelevant to the specific travel service being requested.

In this scenario, passengers—specifically those requesting access to women-only cabins—can share their verified identity data, including their gender attribute, without disclosing any additional, non-essential details (such as home address, nationality, or place of birth). By leveraging the EUDI Wallet's capabilities, Trenitalia can confirm eligibility for gender-specific accommodations while maintaining the highest standard of user privacy and data parsimony throughout the booking process.
