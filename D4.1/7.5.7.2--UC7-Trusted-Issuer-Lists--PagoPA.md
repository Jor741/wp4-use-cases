#### Trusted Issuer Lists

Within the scope of UC7, Trenitalia can verify passenger credentials stored in the PagoPA EUDI Wallet since the issuer is officially recognized within the Pilot’s Trusted Issuer List. This framework allows Trenitalia to programmatically confirm that the PID and European Disability Card  originate from an authorized entity, **Istituto Poligrafico e Zecca dello Stato (IPZS)**.

The integrity and authenticity of these credentials are ensured through a robust cryptographic process:
- Every credential is validated using public keys and digital signatures, which certify the data's trustworthiness and its origin at the time of issuance.
- As the official Italian Public Issuer, IPZS is the authorized authority for issuing identity attestations that Relying Parties, such as Trenitalia, can verify with absolute certainty.

To ensure a secure testing environment and avoid any conflicts with production systems, IPZS will issue these credentials using **mock data**. However, the technical workflow, the underlying protocols, and the verification logic remain identical to the future production environment. This ensures that the pilot remains a realistic and scalable representation of the final **eIDAS 2.0 ecosystem**.
