## UC 6: European Student Experience / Student Access via EUDI Wallet (iDAKTO / PagoPA)

UC6 objective is to enable **students to easily prove their student status in order to access services and benefits** (e.g. transport, accommodation, cultural offers, university services). For instance an Italian Student can access to personalized services in France by using their national EUDIW, provided by PagoPA, and a French student can access the same services in Italy, by using their national Wallet, provided by ANTS.

Currently, students face fragmented and inconsistent processes across providers and countries, often requiring physical cards, multiple documents, or manual verification. Student credentials are not always recognised cross-border, creating friction and limiting access to benefits. EUDIW, students can hold a recognised student credential (e.g. digital student card) and seamlessly present it when needed. This removes the need for manual verification and enables trusted, standardised validation.

UC6 contributes to WP4, specifically for the third **Scenario: Student Access**, demonstrating how EUDIW could streamline the entire student experience cross-border, especially for during Erasmus programs, digitalizing student attestation and enabling them to share it with Service Providers both in university and across the country.

As a result, students across Europe can access services and benefits in a simpler, more consistent, and interoperable way.
